My Story

When I was 12, I immigrated to the United States, following my parents dream of becoming a medical professional. I didn't understand of a lot of the cultural norms and figurative speeches until I integrated into the U.S. school system. Since I was very young, I've been a massive fan of Japanese culture and Anime, and I used that as a way to bridge the gap between me and my classmates. Instead of living the Filipino nurse stereotype, I decided to move to San Francisco to pursue a different path. I juggled school and work to pay for my rent, living expenses, and school tuition. I became a leader of multiple organizations. Most importantly, I received a B.A. in Psychology in just 3.5 years.

Along the way...

As I steadily began to learn more about myself, I jumped around a lot between different career goals - from becoming a Pharmacist, to Psychologist, to Behavioral Analyst, and even as a Recruiter. I wasn't satisfied. After learning more about the Tech Industry, I pivoted one last time.
